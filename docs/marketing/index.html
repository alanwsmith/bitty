why

Why Workshop

Answering the question:

Why do I even need this if I already know javascript?

  • That's a bit like asking why you need a car when you can walk. Both will get you where you need to go eventually, but for lots of cases the car makes way more sense.

  • working without a library/framework is a pain. you spend half your time connecting thing.

  • plain javascript is verbose which slows everything down.

  • huge amounts of javascript is connection boilerplate that connects events to elements with via listeners and selectors. bitty eliminates that work.

  • feature turn around is faster.
